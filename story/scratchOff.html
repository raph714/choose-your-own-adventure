"But first let's make sure I have to go," he said as he pulled his lucky 2069 $5 coin out of his pocket and begain rubbing the scratch-off. The card began to hum a suspensful sounding tune as he went, going up in pitch each time he uncovered a lazer icon. As he moved to the final row, colors began to flash and a triumphant tune sounded.

"Whoa, haven't heard one do that before, what'd you win?" Kumar, hotdog in hand peered over the counter at the card, trying to read it upside down.

Jason laughed, "12 dollars. Welp, time to go to work, catch you tomorrow." The scratch-off chimed that his money had been deposited in his bank account, and he tossed in the garbage on the way out. Still chuckling, he stepped off the curb while turning his collar to the rain, and got hit by a truck, killing him instantly.
